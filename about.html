We are a program that matches journalists with high school students to help them
improve their functional writing skills and open them up to the opportunities afforded
within the field of journalism. Journalists and students alike are invited to apply to
the program through our website. The application form is brief and serves as a way
for us to learn about our customers and their fields of interest. Accepted
students will be matched with a professional in a semester long mentorship program that
focuses on guiding the participants through writing assignments,
discussion, and feedback from peers and professionals. Students will have the
chance to publish their writing on our website.
