Out-of-work journalists looking to find meaningful work are invited to apply for a mentorship
position through JournalistxEdu. Accepted mentors will work with up to ten students
over a semester-long period and will recieve a stipend as compensation for sharing their expertise.
A list of benchmarks that students are expected to reach will be provided
to mentors as a guide for them in creating a mini-curriculum. Mentors are encouraged
to structure their lessons around the disciplines they are most passionate
about. Weekly meetings with students, lesson plans, and writing thoughtful feedback will
comprise the time commitment.
