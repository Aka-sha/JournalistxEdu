Students from all over the country are invited to apply to the JournalistxEdu program! Accepted
participants are matched with a professional mentor whom they will spend one-on-one time
with to receive personalized feedback on their writing. There will also be opportunities
for group discussion and peer review, allowing students to communally synthesize information
and listen to diverse perspectives. Students are expected to commit one and a half
hours per week to face-to-face interaction, in addition to time spent working on weekly
writing assignments.
